What Are The Causes and Impacts of Climate Change?

Welcome to our project about Climate Change. We decided to study climate change because it is something that has a large effect on our society, and we think it is very important that we raise awareness around the issue. Our visualizations tell the story of temperature change, fossil fuel output, CO2 emissions, and natural disasters over the years. Our hopes for this project is that viewers will begin to understand our effect on our climate, and begin to understand the impact of our actions.
